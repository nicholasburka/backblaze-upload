Mold Photo Uploader - Cornell Tech x JustFix

Hi, we're a team of 4 technologists and researchers at Cornell Tech, working with JustFix to see how we can technologically support low-income tenants and housing justice groups in securing speedy repairs for potentially hazardous housing issues. We've identified housing mold as a place we could potentially provide help, by creating a tool that uses Computer Vision Artificial Intelligence to attempt to identify whether a photo contains hazardous mold. We're currently collecting an initial housing mold dataset and would appreciate any photos of mold you can provide, as well as any metadata about the photos. Anything helps!

Thank you!
